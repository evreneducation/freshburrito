About the Franchise

Own and Operate a Fresh Burrito of Your Own!

BACKGROUND

On June 17,2015, fresh burrito opened its door for the first time on fairway road in Kitchener, Ontario. The Mexican inspired concept came into existence with the sole purpose of serving customers home style Mexican food with fresh and healthy ingredients and that's why its name as Fresh Burrito Mexican Grill. Our philosophy is simple, we believe when quality and freshness come first, a great experience will follow. Cool and fresh is the fresh way. Following the simple formula that "Fresh is Healthy", Fresh Burrito quickly became very popular with long lineups of loyal customers.

FUTURE EXPANSION

Over the next 6-month span, Fresh Burrito is opening 5 new franchised units and is focusing on all of Ontario and other provinces . The chain will concentrate on high traffic and highly visible street locations (where possible). Street units require approximately 1000 to 1300 square feet.

Excellent income. Featuring the highest net profit of any full-service restaurant (over 15% profit on net sales).

  • Simple and easy operations, Streamlined, efficient, perfect UP-trending food model.
  • Strong support.

  • long term clean leases (up to 15 years) and provide comprehensive initial and ongoing training.

Franchising FAQ's

  • 1. HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO OWN AND OPEN MY OWN FRESH BURRITO

    Initial Franchise Fees of $25000 plus HST plus the Build Out Cost.
    Turn Key Build Out Cost varies approx. from ($280,000 to $325,000) and is based on the size, location and scope of work.

  • 2. HOW MUCH CASH WILL I NEED TO INVEST?

    For example, if your startup costs are $280,000 (you will need to invest a minimum of $90,000 Approximately in cash in order for the bank to finance the remaining $190,000.

  • 3. DO YOU PROVIDE FINANCING

    Fresh Burrito does not provide financing but all Canadian banks provide financing to qualified Fresh Burrito Franchisees. Once a site has been leased, Fresh Burrito will provide you with direction on how to prepare a professional business plan for the bank.

  • 4. WHY YOUR INVESTMENT COST AND MENU PRICES ARE LOWER THAN THE OTHER BURRITO CONCEPTS IN THE INDUSTRY

    We pass on our costs for equipment, signs, food supplies and packaging directly to you without any markup. Fresh Burrito works with contractors and provide them regular business that's how it's able to negotiate the best price for leasehold improvements from the contractors for construction. This lower initial investment cost means lower start-up debt, which leads directly to greater and faster profitability.

  • 5. HOW MUCH ARE YOUR ROYALITIES?

    Franchisees contribute 5% of gross sales in corporate royalties and an additional 2% towards the advertising fund.

  • 6. WHAT ARE THE NEXT STEPS?

    Email us at info@freshburrito.ca and A Fresh Burrito Representative will contact and follow up with you to discuss your qualification for a Fresh Burrito franchisee and If you don't receive a call or an email within 24-48 hours, please call 416-417-7732 or 647-896-0859 to arrange for a conversation.
    Based on the outcome of that conversation, If you like to continue we will mail you a Questionnaire and a Personal Finance statement and once you return the completed Questionnaire and Personal Finance Statement to our Head Office by email: info@freshburrito.ca A Fresh Burrito Representative will contact and follow up with you to discuss and comment upon our review of your application.
    Based on the outcome of that conversation, you may then meet with the representative in one of our restaurants so that you can get to know each other and we will answer all of your questions.
    Also, at that meeting Fresh Burrito will provide you with a Disclosure Document. This is an in-depth disclosure of information that is material to your decision to invest in a Fresh Burrito franchise. You cannot sign any contracts nor will Fresh Burrito accept any payment until a minimum of two weeks or 14 days have expired after your receipt of this document.

  • 7. HOW DO I FIND FRESH BURRITO RESTUARANT SITE?

    We will work together to find a suitable site or offer you sites that are available or if you have a site in mind, bring it to us and we will research to see if it falls under our guidelines of suitable location. Once you and Fresh Burrito have mutually agreed on the site, we will negotiate the lease and sign with the Landlord. You will sublet from us under exactly the same terms and conditions or lease directly with the landlord as the case may be.

  • 8. WHERE DO I BUY THE FOOD, PACKAGES AND OTHER SUPPLIES?

    Fresh Burrito has contracted with Major Canadian Food Distributor to supply everything you need to operate your restaurant.

  • 9. CAN I OWN MORE THAN ONE FRESH BURRITO LOCATION?

    YES, FOR SURE! But we recommend waiting for alteast 6 months – 1 year of operating your first location as we like to see our Franchisees putting the experience gained from their first success to work on their subsequent successes.
